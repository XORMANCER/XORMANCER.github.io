Welcome to the XOR Grimoire, a reverse engineering and CTF blog.

Security research can be a difficult field to get into, as most sources of knowledge only cover legacy attacks or will hide modern techniques behind paywalls of expensive course material. Industry legends such as Corelan have been monumental in teaching a new generation of reverse engineers, and I am striving to create a similar platform as I progress in my career.

My goal is to assist any aspiring low-level wizards by creating in-depth walkthroughs to better our novice community. While I am still a beginner myself, I hope to give back to those who have paved the way, as we are all standing on the shoulders of giants.

– XORMANCER
